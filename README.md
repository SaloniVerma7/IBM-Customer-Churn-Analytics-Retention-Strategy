# IBM Customer Churn Analytics & Retention Strategy

### End-to-end Customer Churn Analysis using SQL, Python, Machine Learning, and Power BI to identify churn drivers, predict high-risk customers, analyze revenue at risk, and provide actionable retention strategies through interactive dashboards.

## Business Problem

#### Telecom companies face high customer churn which leads to:

Revenue loss from high-value customers
High customer acquisition cost
Low customer lifetime value
No early churn detection
Weak retention strategy

#### The goal is to:

Identify customers likely to churn
Understand churn drivers
Detect high-value customers at risk
Improve retention rate
Reduce revenue loss

## Business Solution

#### This project builds a data-driven churn intelligence system:

SQL-based churn analysis
Customer segmentation (High / Medium / Low Risk)
Revenue at risk identification
Machine Learning churn prediction model
Power BI interactive dashboards
High-value customer churn detection

This helps businesses take proactive retention actions.

## Key Insights

📉 Overall churn rate is 26.5%
📊 Month-to-month contracts contribute ~45% of churn
⚠️ Customers with tenure < 6 months show highest churn risk
💳 Electronic check users churn 25% more than auto-pay users
🌐 Fiber optic customers show highest churn (~35%)
💰 High monthly charge customers show 30–40% higher churn
👤 New customers contribute majority of churn
🔥 High-value customers create maximum revenue loss
📈 Retention rate identified as 73.5%
